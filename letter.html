Dear Ziya ❤️

Aaj ka din mere liye bahut special hai, kyunki aaj us ladki ka birthday hai jo meri life ko aur bhi khoobsurat bana deti hai.

Shayad main hamesha apni feelings ko perfect words mein express nahi kar pata, lekin aaj main dil se kuch kehna chahta hoon.

Jab bhi tum smile karti ho, pata nahi kyun mera din automatically better ho jata hai.

Tumhari baatein, tumhari care, aur tumhara hona hi mere liye bahut special hai.

Main bas itna chahta hoon ki tum hamesha khush raho, haste raho, aur life mein jo bhi chaho woh sab tumhe mile.

Tumhari har wish poori ho, aur tumhari zindagi pyaar, khushiyon aur success se bhari rahe.

Thank you itni amazing person hone ke liye. Aur thank you meri life ka ek beautiful part banne ke liye.

Tum bahut precious ho ❤️

Happy Birthday Ziya 🎂✨

— Sahil ❤️
