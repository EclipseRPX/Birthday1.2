💌 A Letter For My Brother

From Badal To Sahil ❤️

Dear Sahil,


Happy Birthday, bhai. 🎂❤️


Aaj tera birthday hai, aur mujhe laga ki normal message se zyada tu kuch special deserve karta hai. Isliye maine ye poori website sirf tere liye banayi hai.


Jab hum bachche the aur ghar ke bahar saath khela karte the, tab hume bilkul idea nahi tha ki ye dosti itni lambi chalegi. Lekin aaj jab main peeche mudkar dekhta hoon, to realize hota hai ki meri life ki bahut si best memories tere saath judi hui hain.


Lagbhag 10 saal ki dosti koi chhoti baat nahi hoti. Is dauran bahut log mile, bahut log chale gaye, lekin tu hamesha meri life ka important part bana raha.


Tu sirf mera best friend nahi hai. Tu un logon me se hai jin par main bina soche trust kar sakta hoon. Aur aaj ke time me trust sabse valuable cheez hoti hai.


Mujhe hamari woh random baatein, woh hasi-mazaak, woh planning, aur woh sab moments yaad hain jinhe shayad hum dono ne us waqt normal samjha tha, lekin aaj wahi memories priceless lagti hain.


Ek aur baat jo mujhe bahut pasand hai, woh ye hai ki hum sirf past ke baare me baat nahi karte. Hum future ke sapne bhi saath dekhte hain.


Main genuinely hope karta hoon ki ek din hum dono milkar kuch bada create karein. Sirf successful business nahi, balki aisi success jo hume aur hamari families ko proud feel karaye.


Aur chahe future me kitni bhi success mile, kitni bhi luxury cars aa jaayein, kitna bhi paisa aa jaaye, mujhe umeed hai ki hum kabhi nahi bhoolenge ki sab kuch kahan se shuru hua tha — do dost, apne ghar ke bahar khelte hue.


Thank you for being a part of my life. Thank you for all the memories. Thank you for being someone I can trust.


Aaj ke din main bas itna kehna chahta hoon:


No matter what happens, no matter where life takes us, you'll always be my brother first and my business partner second.


Stay happy. Stay healthy. Keep chasing your dreams. And never stop being the amazing person you are.


Happy Birthday once again, bhai. 🎉❤️



— Badal 🤝
